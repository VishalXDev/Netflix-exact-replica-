Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices.

You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single ad – all for one low monthly price. There’s always something new to discover, and new TV shows and movies are added every week!

How much does Netflix cost?

Netflix has a variety of plans to suit your needs. Check the official website for the latest pricing information.

Where can I watch?

You can watch Netflix on any internet-connected device, such as smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, set-top boxes, smartphones, tablets, and PCs.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel your Netflix membership online at any time. There are no cancellation fees.
